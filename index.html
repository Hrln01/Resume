Dear Hiring Manager,

Today I am here to explain why I would relish this opportunity and why I am the right choice.

I possess all the standard qualities you would seek: strong communication skills, adaptability, professionalism, and intelligence. However, what truly distinguishes me is my unrelenting drive to succeed. I firmly believe, as I trust you do, that the key to any success in sales is an innate hunger—a delusional ambition that outweighs all else, as all other requirements of a role are easily attainable after this.

My Story:

I was raised very old school, under the roof of a disciplined salesman; in hindsight I am lucky. It was a given that I have ventures spanning over a decade, starting with classic lemonade stands, selling crystals and toys at school, and lolly hustling in high school, then onto e-commerce and B2B wholesale shortly after; I’ve been immersed in this culture for a long time.

My most noteworthy success was in the Fast-Moving-Consumable-Goods. In early 2022, starting with a mere $400, I gradually learned through sheer experience; the whole life cycle of a sale in great detail, and many critical aspects of business, most importantly the art of hustle—from direct-to-consumers and later onto B2B partnerships. Over time, I eventually touched weekly revenue that saw over $50,000, achieving this through B2B channels and thousands of independent consumers.

Nearing the regulatory changes on Electronic Nicotine Delivery Systems, of course, I knew there would be a massive void in the market; after careful consideration and research on any new regulatory changes, I pivoted. Very quickly, I saw over $25,000 in sales a week after a period of limbo, primarily through B2B channels. It wasn’t the same money, but it was the best option at that time; I was simply happy to have found an alternative.

It was a truly great experience having these distribution businesses; I also gained insight into the inner workings of other businesses I sold to. Everyone’s interconnected; be fair, make them feel special, connect, and with a decent product, it can spread like wildfire within their own network. I became the largest importer and distributor in Western Australia and had people reaching out from NSW and Sydney.

I acknowledge the controversy of the nicotine industry but hope we can see this as a reflection of a proactive approach to seizing rare opportunities in an already very limited space for a 20-year-old. I am now committed to channelling extraordinary ambition into a respected profession. I only operated in both businesses while they had the framework that meant it could be sold legally as an NRT product.

I may not come from a traditional background, but I do have a fire to win, and I’ve got a history of experience to back it up—this makes me uniquely qualified. I won’t be there to clock in and out; I will be there to push boundaries and drive growth. I can and will adapt quickly, master my responsibilities, and let my work consume my mind.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Thank you for your time.
Best regards,
Harlen
